Visit Ferizaj

Enjoy culture and the beautiful nature in this vibrant city. Here you will find beautiful parks, great bars and delicious food.

Top three activities to do at Ferizaj

Go for a walk in Liria Park

The pine trees in the park area of 42 hectares were planted in 1952. The park has walking paths, lighting and seats. Liria Park serves citizens as a space to spend moments to rest and relaxation.

Take a look at beautiful great murals

All around the city you will be enchanted by beautiful artistic murals. One of them is the greatest mural of the famous albanian singer Rita Ora.

Visit the bifurcation of river Nerodime

Take a walk around the bifurcation of river Nerodime, enjoy the quite water and peaceful nature. Here you can stop for a coffee break and have a lunch in the beautiful garden.
